HOW DOES THIS WORK

When you adopt Rapid Compiler to digitalize your result compilation process, your

teachers would be able to progressively enter students' assessments through the

course of the term, at their comfort, using just mobile devices

Compiler performs all the magic (calculation) needed for compiling the results

and generates the result for students in your school, based on your selected result formats.
